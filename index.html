About Youth Red Cross

The Youth Red Cross is a humanitarian organization dedicated to promoting health, social welfare, and community service among young people. It operates under the principles of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement and strives to create a culture of empathy, compassion, and volunteerism among youth.

Through various programs and initiatives, the Youth Red Cross engages in activities such as blood donation drives, first aid training, disaster relief, and community outreach. Our mission is to empower young individuals to make a positive impact on society and contribute to the well-being of those in need.
